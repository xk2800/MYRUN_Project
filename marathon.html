Running a marathon is one of the most challenging physical activities one can choose to participate in. Runners who run the marathon often train for several months prior to the race and endure mental and physical pain during and after the race. However, to persevere and complete a marathon is considered one of the most rewarding personal experiences. This race is ideal for runners who run and train on a regular basis, as well as runners looking to challenge themselves. Individuals, who are new to the sport of running, and who have not trained or run in a distance race before, should refrain from participating in the marathon as their first choice of race discipline.
