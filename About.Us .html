The 1st edition of the race will carry the FUTURISTIC theme, where the year 2020 will be the year that we envisaged to start an era of transformation and digitalisation. We too as an organiser will be ready in gearing up to adapt and bring those changes to runners.


It is also symbolises growth and improvement, our promise from the 1st edition to keep improving in every aspect of organising MyRun Marathon.

So, let's together run MyRun Marathon 2020 and support the vision that will propel the country into a much greater heights!
