AGE REQUIREMENT

For safety reasons, there is an age limit for each race distance category.

A participant for the Veteran or the Malaysia Veteran must be 40 years old or older as of race day.

Please note that the online registration form will automatically restrict the entry, based on your date of birth.

Race Category Age Requirement
Full Marathon Participants for the Marathon must be 18 years old or older on Sunday 14 June 2021
Half Marathon Participants for the Half Marathon must be 16 years old or older on Sunday 14 June 2021
10 KM Participants for the 10 km races must be 15 years old or older on Saturday 13 June 2021
5 KM Participants for the 5 km must be 12 years old or older on Saturday 13 June 2021
